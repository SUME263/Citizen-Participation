About the Platform

The Citizen Participation platform is designed to bridge the gap between governments and citizens. With this platform, you can actively engage in public governance, make your opinions heard, and contribute to your community's development.

Key Features

User Registration

Sign up easily using a government-issued ID or email and start participating in your community's decisions.

Feedback and Suggestions

Submit feedback on public policies and projects, upvote ideas, and engage in constructive discussions.

Polls and Voting

Vote on policies, share your opinions, and see how your community feels about critical issues.

Virtual Town Halls

Attend virtual meetings to discuss concerns, ask questions, and interact with government representatives.

Project Tracking

Follow public projects in real time, provide feedback at different stages, and stay informed about progress.

Report Issues

Report local issues like road damage, noise complaints, and more, and track their resolution status.
